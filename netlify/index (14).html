Our Story

Not your average course food

We started The Tap In because golf course food didn't have to mean sad hot dogs and stale chips. We wanted something you'd actually drive to — even if you weren't playing a round.

Everything on our menu is made with intention. Crispy, saucy, hot, fresh. The chicken sandwich people talk about. Chicken & waffles worth stopping for. Cocktails built for a sunny afternoon on the course.

Come hungry. Leave happy. Tell your friends.
